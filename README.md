Automotive Manufacture EV vs. ICE Demand 
Based on recent U.S. vehicle sales and EV adoption trends, should automotive manufacturers prioritize electric vehicles, hybrids, or internal combustion engines to meet future consumer demand?
This question matters because OEM manufacturers must make long term production and investment decisions based on consumer demand, and inaccurate powertrain preferences can result in lost market share and wasted resources.
